What is the NBA?

The logo of the NBA

The NBA logo was designed in 1969 by Alan Siegel. He chose a picture of Jerry West, a famous player, and represent his silhouette in white. He placed it against a red and blue background, forming the logo we still see today. The colors chosen are those of the american flag.

The Regular Season

The NBA's regular season runs from October to April, each team plays 82 games to know which one is going to go to the Playoffs (the first eight teams of each conference).

The Playoffs

The NBA playoffs are a best-of-seven elimination tournament annually held after the National Basketball Association (NBA)’s regular season to determine the league's champion.

For the first time this year, there was a match nba in Paris. It was a historic day because normally every match is in the USA. The NBA chose one team which containing French player as Nicolas Battum (Hornets) and another one which is the superstar Giannis Antetokumpo's team (Bucks).

All Star Game

An all-star game is an exhibition game that purports to showcase the best players (the "stars") of a sports league. The exhibition is between two teams organized solely for the event, usually representing the league's teams based on region or division, in NBA it's the conference.
